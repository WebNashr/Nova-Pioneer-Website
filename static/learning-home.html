Pre-primary

Today's youth will encounter a future that will look and work differently than the world today, and that will continue to evolve during their lifetime.

NovaPioneer’s focus on technological fluency and project-based (hands-on) learning is designed to equip students to tackle challenging, real-world problems as they enter post-secondary education and their professional lives.

Beyond Exams

We ensure that our students excel on their exams, but we go much beyond that–our students engage deeply and think critically about course material, and apply their academic work to real world situations.

Holistic and High Expectations

We support every student to unlock their individual interests and passions. We treat students as leaders and they in turn develop the characters and skills to meet our expectations.

Practical and Personalized

Our learning experiences are full of engagement, creativity, and energy. In and out of the classroom, students apply their knowledge to solve real problems, and learn to work collaboratively in teams with facilitation from our highly trained teachers.

21st Century Skills

Nova Pioneer students don’t just learn how to use the latest technology, they learn how to design and build it themselves. Our laboratories and maker spaces allow students to experiment with their own inventions, learning technology tools that they’ll need to thrive in modern careers.

Whether they're passionate about art, technology, science, or music creation - we help develop students' interests and nurture their talents so that they can achieve far beyond what they thought was possible.

Primary

At Nova Pioneer, problem solving activities allow learners the opportunity to "try-first". Our lesson structure allows students to try solving unfamiliar problems before working with teachers to clarify the underlying concepts and skills related to the problems they have solved.

This opportunity promotes students to engage with one another, build teamwork and critical thinking skills and increase confidence levels that will allow them to solve unknown problems and challenges related to real-life situations.

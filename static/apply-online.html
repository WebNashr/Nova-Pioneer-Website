Nova Pioneer Application

Nova Pioneer prepares students to be the future leaders and innovators of Kenya and the world.

Applicants who are strong candidates for Nova Academies are those who excel academically, who exhibit a love of learning, who have a demonstrated a passion for technology and the sciences, and who have developed strong interests in extracurricular activities including music, sports, and drama. Most importantly, the admissions committee looks for scholars who model the values and character that are core to the Nova philosophy: generosity, responsibility, courage, and excellence in their daily lives.

Please fill out the form below to apply to Nova Academies. Kindly note that candidates will be evaluated and admissions decisions will be made according to the following schedule:

Round 1 Round 2 Round 3 Round 4 Round 5
Submit Application by: 12th April 2nd July 30th September 18th November 8th December
Assessed by: 22nd April 9th July 6th October 25th November 15th December
Notification Date: 15th June 1st September 12th October 1st December 19th December

After filling out the application, you will receive a confirmation email shortly notifying you receipt of the application. We will contact qualified applicants regarding next steps. Submission of this form does not guarantee acceptance into Nova Academies – we have a limited places in our first class and we will further assess applicants to determine if they meet the criteria for those places.

We currently only accept applications for scholars entering Form 1 in 2017.

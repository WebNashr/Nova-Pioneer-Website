Working at Nova Pioneer

Building the future of African talent and global education

Why work at NovaPioneer?

Do life changing things with life changing people. Our teachers and staff are innovative, hard-working, and high achieving men and women with a healthy disregard for what is considered to be impossible. At NovaPioneer, you have the support of an entire network dedicated to professional development supported by our teaching model. We are looking for passionate people who are excited to impact the future of Africa through their work today.

Whether you seek to grow your teaching career or support teachers and students outside of the classroom, you will find exceptional support and growth at Nova Pioneer in a network of people who are motivated to make a difference through education.

Education Pathways

At Nova Pioneer we recognize and value different leadership pathways. We do not box you in. Over your career with Nova Pioneer you may chart a personal path through several of these roles. With plans to open 100 schools over the next ten years – and an emphasis on developing and hiring from within – there will be plenty of advancement opportunities for staff who are equipped to succeed in them. At Nova Pioneer we recognize and value that different educators hold different leadership aspirations. We expect growth and increasing leadership from everyone, and offer a variety of paths for that.

School leader

For educators who aspire to lead a school.

Master Educator

Stay in the classroom as a Lead Teacher, working with students and mentoring junior teachers to mastery.

Network Specialist

For lead teachers who want to stay closely engaged in teaching and learning but seek to lead beyond their teaching team. This could range from coaching teachers across our schools, to pioneering improvements in an area of our work. (e.g. learning technology, project-based learning, leadership development, African studies)

Business Builder

for a few colleagues who want to step into an organizational development role with the NovaPioneer central team. We value teaching experience in our central operations and will support the transition to business builder with training and mentorship.

Admission Process

Developing innovators & leaders who will shape the future

1

Apply Online

Did you know? We Process applications all year around, so don't wait until you've taken your KCPEs because spots fill up fast!

2

Book Your Interview

Once we've reviewed your application, you'll get a call from one of our admissions officers inviting your for an interview and assessment on our campus.

3

Interview and Assessment

We administer our own internal assessments, which includes an interview with one of our staff members, a written English and Math exam, and an interactive activity. We use this data to determine whether or not the student will be admitted.

4

Next Steps

When you come for your interview and assessment, our admissions team will notify you as to when you can expect to hear back from us about your status. We accept students on a "rolling basis", so the sooner that you are assessed the sooner you'll hear back from our team.

Frequently Asked Questions

  • How will students who are coming from a school with a different learning model adapt at Nova Pioneer?

    At Nova, our students learn through embracing and eventually overcoming challenges with the help of their peers, their teachers, and developing their unique abilities. Students from every background are not only welcome, but will learn to thrive in Nova’s academic approach. Our impressive teaching staff is trained to treat each student in a highly personalized way, helping him uncover his unique talents and passions.

  • How do you manage career guidance?

    Our students don’t just learn theory, they work on real-world problems in a hands-on way to prepare them for life as professionals and entrepreneurs. At Nova, students have the opportunity to work with successful companies on exciting projects that help them shape their personal career goals. Then, they work with our highly trained coaches and mentors to talk about their passions and embark on the path towards achieving their ambitions.

  • How are parents involved in their children’s education?

    We believe that parents are one of the most important forces that shapes a child’s experience. Nova Pioneer parents are involved in their child’s education and have the opportunity to contribute to school life and community. We keep you updated as to your child’s progress, and tap into your experience through career days and special events. We consider our founding parent network to be integral to developing the optimal school experience for each student.

  • How can I be sure that my child's well being is protected when he is boarding?

    Each child’s well-being and development is our main concern and every measure will be taken to ensure transparency and safety. All Nova Pioneer staff are trained and monitored constantly. The matron and boarding staff are in charge of the dorms and closely track each child’s well being. In addition, Nova Pioneer has house keepers, guards and nurses and counsel on duty, and we have security patrolling at all times.

  • What kind of catering will there be?

    We are very cognisant of creating healthy, delicious meals that fuel the minds and bodies of our students. Our meals are be well-balanced and nutritious, but not fancy.

  • Is the school Christian based or secular?

    We are an inclusive school and support all students to live and practice their faith in and outside the school. Nova Pioneer is open to students of all faiths and we will support each student’s spiritual needs. There is dedicated prayer time during the day for those who practice. During the weekends, students are able to engage with their faith, by attending a nearby church on Sunday or the mosque on Friday.

  • How will you be integrating diverse cultures and socio economic groups at Nova Pioneer?

    At Nova Pioneer, we believe that diversity is critical to developing a sophisticated world view, and part of the Nova learning approach is to learn from others who may have a different perspective from your own. We believe that education should be inclusive and equitable, and we seek students from all backgrounds and talents to contribute to our community.

  • What is the background of your teachers?

    At the core of a great school are great teachers. We are incredibly selective in recruiting top talent. Our teachers come from top Kenyan institutions including Maseno, Mang’u and Alliance, but we don’t stop there. All of our teachers undergo rigorous, immersive training, and ongoing professional development is continually supported and monitored.

  • How will you handle incidents of bullying?

    Nova Pioneer teaches all students to be mature, responsible, respectful individuals and bullying of any sort is not tolerated. We take bullying offenses extremely seriously and any student found bullying will be expelled.

About Nova Pioneer

Developing innovators & leaders who will shape the future

Our Vision

Nova Pioneer’s unique approach to education will make you reimagine what is possible for your child. Rooted in decades of learning science, and best models in the world, ours is a student-centric approach that develops leaders and innovators who are equipped to shape the world they envision.



Our Mission

Nova Pioneer (South Africa) and Nova Pioneer (East Africa) are part of the NovaPioneer Education Group. NovaPioneer is an independent school network offering excellent and affordable enquiry-based education that is focused on both the cognitive and emotional development of students. Our schools offer pre-school through secondary education for students from ages 3 through 19.

We established Nova Pioneer because we believe education should prepare young people to create their future, not just work in it. Nowhere is that more important than in Africa. By 2050 40% of the world’s youth will live in Africa. In less than 100 years, 40% of the world’s total population, young and old, will live in Africa. Nova Pioneer will serve as a launchpad where thousands of young Africans can begin to shape the world for better.

Our founders began by asking themselves the question, "What capabilities will young people need to succeed in and shape the future that they will encounter, and what approaches to education will best develop those capabilities?”

We visited and studied innovative and leading schools spanning from South Africa to Singapore, Sweden to Peru, Italy to India, and the United States to the United Kingdom. We connected with those of the front lines of cutting edge education research, and leading thinkers on 21st Century Learning including Sir Ken Robinson, the Clayton Christensen Institute, IDEO’s David Kelley and Sandy Speicher, teachers and atelierista’s from Reggio Emilia, to name a few.

We then asked “how do we make that exceptional education as affordable and accessible as possible?”

Over the next 20 years, 100 million children will become young adults across Africa. That is more than the rest of the world combined. Each one of those young Africans dreams a great dream for themselves – a dream of achieving success and of making a positive contribution to the world. We started Nova Pioneer to equip as many of those youth as we can to realise their great dream.

We are developing generations of innovators and leaders who will shape the African Century. Over the next 10 years, we intend to transform the lives and career paths of more than 50,000 youths across 100 schools, spanning all three regions of sub-Saharan Africa. Our graduates’ exceptional experiences in our schools and their achievements beyond school will change expectations of schooling across our continent, earning Nova Pioneer the influence to lead positive change in public and private schooling across Africa.

School & Company Culture

Culture first: We believe that you cannot have great learning or build a great community without a great and positive culture. At NovaPioneer we live and learn by the following six culture principles.

Enquiry: We foster a curiosity and a love of learning, a commitment to deep understanding, and learning how to think and how to learn.

  • Joy of learning: We delight in learning and bring infectious energy and positive spirit to everything we do because joy fuels learning and hard work.
  • Always growing: We constantly seek out difficult challenges, share and receive feedback as a gift, and see every failure as an opportunity to learn and grow.

Caring: We care about each other, about ourselves, and the world in which we live.

  • Greater Together: We collaborate with and support our colleagues and students because we can achieve more together.
  • Servant Leadership: We believe great leaders are great servants, and put service to others before service to self.

Excellence: We hold high expectations of each other and ourselves, and of the quality of our work.

  • High expectations (110%): We see all our students, colleagues, and ourselves as capable of greatness, and we expect everyone to do whatever it takes to constantly achieve excellence.
  • Solutions first (Optimism): We respond to every obstacle with energy and action and always bring solutions when we raise problems.
